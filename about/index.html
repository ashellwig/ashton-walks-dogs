About Me

Hi! My name is Ashton Scott Hellwig. I live in Lafayette, Colorado with my lovely wife Victoria Sanchez. While we do not currently have any dogs of our own, we both have owned many, many dogs in the past. I personally have had 1 Siberian Husky, 1 Alaskan Husky, 1 Yorkipoo, 1 Jack Russel Terrier, 3 Poodles, and 1 Austrailian Shepherd.

My experience with animals began when I was 12 years old. This was the minimum age that was allowed to volunteer the Georgia Small Dog Rescue in Atlanta, GA. There I was an adoption specialist. In Atlanta I also earned all of my equine experience. I volunteered at a barn close by my home outside of the main city.

Currently, I am both a dog walker/sitter for WagWalking as well as a sourcing agent for Mrs. Green's Remedies. I am also a college sophomore at Front Range Community College where I switched my major from Chemistry to Mathematics, and now Computational Mathematics for a career in veterinary bioinformatics.
